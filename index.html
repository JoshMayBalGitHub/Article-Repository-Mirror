Advocating for Good Mental Health

Written by: JoshMayBalloon

Every young children can display early signs of mental health issues. These mental health issues are frequently clinically diagnosable and can be the result of biological psychological, and social factors. Half of all mental health disorders show first signs before a person turns 14 years old, and three-quarters of mental health disorders begin before age 24.
